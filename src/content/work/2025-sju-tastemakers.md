From an article celebrating 50 years of Saint Joe's field hockey program to reflections on how our campus locations will honor Pope Francis' Laudato Si’ declaration, the Spring 2025 edition of the Saint Joseph's University Magazine was packed with rich storytelling and great features. And one story in particular, the [Saint Joseph's University TasteMakers Food Tour](https://www.sju.edu/news/magazine/tastemakers), stole the show.

In addition to the interviews and storytelling, this digital package also included a 5-part video series ala Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives as well as 30+ custom illustrations and a mapping feature displaying locations of all the businesses in question.

Our biggest challenge was figuring out how to tie all these diverse elements together while allowing them to breathe on their own.

My design for a custom video banner teasing each episode but letting users experience the article content first was a creative solution that helped balance out the importance of each section of the page. In addition I developed a new slideshow carousel that can accommodate various aspect ratios, a side-by-side component that can contain different kinds of media, and a story hero that can be as unique as each story requires.

To date, Tastemakers Food Tour has been one of our best performing magazine features, bringing users to the site both organically and through targeted marketing efforts. It's even been integrated into our in-person open house events as well as a lecture series hosted by the Haub School of Business.

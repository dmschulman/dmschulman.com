After such a successful launch of our first all-digital university magazine in 2023, our team was anxious to get cracking on the next edition.

For this issue our teams were tasked with highlighting the [fantastic research and scholarly activity our campus centers and institutes](https://www.sju.edu/news/magazine/front-center) undertake throughout the year. Saint Joseph's University currently counts 7 unique organizations that pursue such activity including the Pedro Arrupe Center for Business Ethics, Center for Addiction and Recovery Education (CARE), Institute for Clinical Bioethics (ICB), Faith-Justice Institute, Institute for Jewish-Catholic Relations (IJCR), Institute for Environmental Stewardship, and The Kinney Center for Autism Education and Support.

The package included articles for each center featuring their highlights and impact, quotes from researchers and educators involved in each of the institutes, and unique photo-illustrations visually conveying the mission of each organization. Custom link cards were developed to allow the visuals to stand out and entice users to click deeper into the package.

In a similar vein, our other headline story [profiled Jason Wallach, PhD](https://www.sju.edu/news/magazine/psychedelic-renaissance) and the work him and his lab team have been pioneering in the area of therapeutic psychedelics research. Dr. Wallach has become a nationally recognized figure for his work studying the 5-HT2A receptor and NMDA receptor antagonists and his involvement with the Saint Joseph's Philadelphia College of Pharmacy.

Wallach and his team, among other important discoveries, have sought to patent more than 200 compounds developed at his specialized campus lab. As the article fit a standard format, it was our job to pull a design together that would let the content breathe and give us a page template for future features. Pull quote components were developed to showcase salient details and a mind-bending graphic treatment was conceived to give the package the appropriate vibe.

In this edition we also, to much fanfare, [unveiled our inaugural 30 Under 30 series](https://www.sju.edu/news/saint-josephs-university-celebrates-30-trailblazing-hawks-under-30) highlighting 30 Saint Joseph's alumni under the age of 30 who are making a difference in their field or within their community. You can read more about my work on 30 Under 30 here.
